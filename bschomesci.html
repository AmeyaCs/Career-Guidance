B.sc in home science

Home Science or the study of home making deals with subjects intimately connected with the daily activities of the home maker such as food, clothing, shelter, finance health, childcare, home beautification, community service etc. To understand the various aspects of home living knowledge of basic sciences and arts subject such as physics, chemistry, biology, economics, sociology, anthropology etc. are necessary.
Home Science has developed into five branches and their specialization are:

Future Scope

The graduate in Home Science can undergo BTC, B.Ed., PG courses and doctoral degrees in its specified areas. They may opt teaching in degree colleges as well as in schools. Some may join International Organisations such as WHO, UNESCO, UNICEF etc. and may also join ICAR, ICMR, NIRD, NIPCCD
