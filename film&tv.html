Eligibility

Duration (1/2 Year's)

Graduates in any discipline and students awaiting their degree final year examination results are eligible to apply for courses in Direction and Cinematography.
Candidates aspiring for Editing and Sound Recording and Designing should have passed Intermediate/Plus Two examination. Editing and Sound Design candidates should possess computer knowledge.
For Digital Filmmaking course, preference will be given to graduate students although those with Intermediate qualification can apply.
