foreign language diploma

This department offers courses in French, German, Russian, Japanese and Spanish at various levels, from elementary to post graduate courses (M.A., M.Phil, Ph.D.) Batches are conducted in the mornings as well as in the evenings at various centers in the city for the convenience of part time students.


The Certificate, Intensive Certificate, Diploma, Intensive Diploma, Advanced Diploma and the Special Diploma courses in all languages are part-time courses held at many centers and at different timings to suit the needs of students and professionals. The post graduate courses offered in French, German and Russian are full time courses. From June 2001 onwards our M.A. courses have incorporated the credit-system by means of which a student will be evaluated on the number of credits he/she has collected during the semester and not by means of an examination. This will enhance inter-disciplinary interaction and the student will have a wider choice of subjects to study from.


The course is designed as a one-year, part-time post graduation diploma. It will provide training in Foreign Language Education (FLE) and emphasise the how of foreign language education, on the basis of both didactic and linguistic theories and practice.


Eligibility

Duration (Depends on language)

B.A. (any subject) and Advanced Diploma in the foreign language concerned with a minimum of 50%, or in an examination recognised as equivalent


B.A. (Hons) in the foreign language concerned with a minimum of 50%. For in-service teachers the minimum requirement in either case will be 40%
