fashion design diploma

Fashion Design is a wide ocean of opportunities and constant evolution- precisely the reason why our 2 year (One Year Fast Track Advance Diploma option available) Fashion Design course lays priority emphasise on practical industry oriented training. This is an intensive course that is driven by a systematic technical approach that stresses on the detailed knowledge of the subject as gained by research and thematic work. In order to match the ever-changing medium of Fashion, the course as conducted by us relies on tracking the evolving trends, norms and inventions of the Indian and International Fashion Industry and synchronising our curriculum in line with it.


While to the lay person, fashion is simply a glamorous field replete with models & gossip, it is a part of the huge garment industry - India's largest private sector employer & forex earner. Multinationals, export houses and fashion companies are recruiting designers for diverse roles.


The Diploma Course in Fashion Designing is a 12 months program that opens the doors to enter Export Houses, Garment Stores, Textile Mills, Boutiques, Jewellery Houses and Media Houses as Fashion Designer, Visual Merchandiser, Fashion Stylist or Fashion Entrepreneur.


Eligibility

Duration (1-4 Year)

Pass with 50% aggregate marks in 10th or equivalent (with English).


Future Scope

Around the world, India is the largest exporter of fabrics and accessories. India’s growing designer wear is propelled through imagination and a strong reasoning with culture. This dynamic field opens many career opportunities for the students in various fields, which include export houses, garment store, textiles mills, boutiques, jewellery houses and media houses. The curriculum of Fashion Design at Image takes students through a series of workshops, which improves their skills in not only becoming a professional but also an entrepreneur.
