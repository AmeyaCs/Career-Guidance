import & expoet Diploma

The students are exposed to a thorough study of the various departments of Export-Import trade. Though the course emphasizes more on the export business, import formalities are also covered. The course covers various export-related topics such as marketing, packaging, shipping, financing, documentation and policies from a practical viewpoint. The curriculum has been designed to cover the latest developments in all these fields and the course material is constantly revised to reflect changes in policies, procedures etc.


The unique programme is designed keeping in mind the needs of the people who are working in operational areas of Export-import Business, but not had the opportunity of undergoing a well designed formal professional programme. Also for the people who aspire for a career in Export-import / International Business. Participant profile include company sponsored / recommended executives / employees, foreign nationals, self-sponsored executives / employees, Entrepreneurs, Professionals and self - sponsored individuals.


The Programme is ideal for


- Company Sponsored / Recommended participants /Promising executives / employees who have been identified by their employers for job enlargement / higher responsibilities.


- Foreign / NRI Participants.


- Senior officers and managers of the Private / Public Organisation.


- Foreign / NRI Participants.


- Officers / Managers of the private / public organisations. Self-sponsored Participants.


- Executives / Employees who aspire to have career advancement in Export-import related areas; have potentials and a urge for formal learning and are committed to effectively discharge duties in the Organisation.


Eligibility

Duration (1,2,3 Year's)

(i) Under Graduate / Diploma Holder in any discipline from an Indian or Foreign university / Institution.

(ii) Work experience desirable but not essential.

(iii) Academic excellence / accomplishments, organisational sponsorships / recommendations etc., may receive due weightage.

Note :

1) Students who are undergoing management / P.G. courses / Engineering / other professional course and final year of graduation . Diploma Holders of any discipline are also eligible to apply.

2) Certificate holders of IIEIM who have scored an aggregate 60% marks and above are eligible to apply.
