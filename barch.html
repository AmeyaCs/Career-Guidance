B-Arch

The Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.) is an undergraduate academic degree designed to satisfy the academic component of professional accreditation bodies, to be followed by a period of practical training prior to professional examination and registration. It is awarded for a course of study that lasts up to five years. In some cases, a B.Arch can be undertaken after having completed a three-year generalized architectural degree plus an additional two years.


The Bachelor of Architecture program aims at attaining a high level of proficiency in architectural design. Studio exercises make the key part of curriculum where students take inputs from faculty, experts from various disciplines and amongst themselves, progressively refining their artistic sensitivity and creative processes. Design critiques and studio exercises make teaching process a collaborative exercise to strengthen intellectual growth and capacity to develop innovative solutions to various design problems.


To be an architect in today’s complex, fast changing and globalized society, the architect must have knowledge beyond the profession. Drawing inspiration from visual arts, humanities and urbanism, the school follows a truly multidisciplinary teaching approach that expands student’s perspective. Students are actively taken on field trips, interact and participate in art camps with artists and sculptors and frequently engage in dialogue with leading practicing architects and other design professionals. Various subjects related to visual design and presentation, computer aided architectural design and building construction are covered to acquire expertise and skill necessary to professionally practice as an architect. 5 year Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.) Degree program comprises of three stages.


Eligibility

Duration (2/5 Year's)

QUALIFICATION: Candidates must have a minimum of 40% marks in the All India National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) conducted by Council of Architecture (CoA) and must have passed any one of the following courses with a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate:

10+2 with Mathematics and English as compulsory subjects

OR

3 years Diploma after 10 years of schooling (any stream) recognized by Central/State Government with Mathematics and English as compulsory subjects.
