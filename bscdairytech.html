B.Sc Dairy Technology

B.Sc. Dairy Technology or Bachelor of Science in Dairy Technology is an undergraduate Agriculture Science and Technology course. Dairy technology is a part of science, dealing with the processing of milk and its products. It deals with the study of packaging, processing, transportation, storage and distribution of dairy products by implying the science of biochemistry, bacteriology and nutrition. Apart from that, some principles of chemistry, physics, engineering and economics are also included in the field of dairy technology, in order to develop the proper utilization of milk and its products. The study also includes various experiments of effects of different kinds of feed, environmental conditions of milk production and several types of research regarding the prevention of wastage and damage of milk and milk products. The course is of three years duration and is job orienting in nature.


Eligibility

Duration (3 Year's)

A candidate is required to pass the Higher Secondary or 10+2 Examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as compulsory subjects to take an entrance test for admission to an undergraduate course in Dairy Technology.


Future Scope

After the foundation of National Dairy Development Board and the occurrence of “Operation Flood”, the demand for industry-ready technologists specialized in the field of Dairy technology has increased manifolds. They are needed to assure that the design, development and execution of various projects aimed at smooth running of the dairy industries of the country continues at its increased pace. There are huge employment opportunities in the country and also in foreign countries such as Australia, Denmark and Switzerland. The aspirants can work as food analyst / lab technician / assistant food chemist / supervisor / food inspector / quality controller / product development head / marketing head / food technologist in numerous diary firms.
