hotel management degree

The Indian hospitality industry has recorded healthy growth fuelled by increased inflow of foreign tourists as well as increased tourist movement within the country and to make it one of the leading players in the global industry, the best Hotel Management Colleges/Institutes in India are required to cater to aspiring students.


A degree in Hotel Management will enable you to take on a variety of hospitality jobs with confidence. Amongst other things, we equip you with background work that relates to language study, financial aspects, and human resources issues in the job.


Eligibility

Duration (3 Year's)

The candidate should have passed 10+2 stage examination of 10+2 system or equivalent examination in any academic or vocational stream with English as a compulsory subject. The candidate selected provisionally will not be allowed to join/continue in case they fail in +2 stage examination or are placed in compartment.
