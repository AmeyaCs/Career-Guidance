The M.Ed. degree is a practitioner's degree. It is a more appropriate option for those who plan careers as instructional designers and technologists.

Upon completion of the Master of Education Program, the graduate will be able to:

discuss learning processes and implications for the development of effective instruction,

conduct comprehensive needs assessments identifying important learner, environmental, and task characteristics,

develop effective instructional materials for a variety of learning tasks, student characteristics, and learning environments,

evaluate the effectiveness of educational materials,

apply these skills to corporate in school environments

Eligibility

Duration (2 Year's)

One needs at least 50% marks in any post graduate stream, be it social sciences, humanities or science. Secondly a minimum of 50% marks in theory and practical’s respectively in B.Ed. or in the education part of the four year B.A, B.Ed. or B.Sc., B.Ed.

There is a separate tuition fee that is charged to students for the entire year. Normally a meagre sum of Rs. 3000 is charged. This can change from institute to institute. The forms can be obtained from the website of the institute or it can be procured directly from the institute. Students should always keep in mind the last date of submission online, by hand, by post etc. As post the due dates admission forms are certainly not accepted.
