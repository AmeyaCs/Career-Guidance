Art teacher Dipoma

Enable the Art Teacher to exert leadership in programme developments in Art for Primary and Secondary Schools to make him sufficiently grounded in both theory and practical subject and professed of the knowledge and skills necessary for teaching the subject in an integrated manner. To enable him to contrive and use variety of effective teaching and learning procedures. He should be complete and willing to experiment with approved and new methods of presenting art experience to children.To enable him to recognize individual needs of students and create teaching and learning situations based on individual needs. He should be skill full in recognizing and fostering creative talent in pupils.


Eligibility

Duration (2 Year's)

Must have passed H.S.C. examination (12th std) conducted by the government of Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Education and Intermediate Drawing Grade Examination conducted by the Directorate of Art, Maharshtra state, Mumbai.
