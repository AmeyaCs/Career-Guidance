amie

AMIE the engineering degree in the distance education mode offered by the Institution of Engineers (India) helps you become a graduate engineer. Jyothis Academy provides complete assistance for admission, registration and study amie course. Supported by a team of educational visionaries, we aim at imparting high quality amie education within the reach of an average student.


Be a diploma or non-diploma holder, employed or un-employed, your dream to become a chartered engineer can be fulfilled through AMIE.


Known for its excellance in amie postal coaching, admission guidance and study materials, Jyothis Academy has won a remarkable position among AMIE students in India. We offer AMIE books, correspondence, regular and part time classes for amie Section A and Section B examinations.


AMIE is an engineering degree equivalent to B.Tech. Successful completion of AMIE enables you get equal opportunities for job and higher education in Govt, and private sectors just like a B.Tech holder.


Eligibility

Duration (6 Year's)

1. A recognized 3-year Polytechnic Diploma or its equivalent in any branch of engineering. (No marks restriction)


2. +2/HSC/PDC with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics with minimum 45% marks. Any educational qualifications equivalent to the above, are also eligible to apply for AMIE.


Future Scope

- You may apply for superior posts.

- You may now work as a Consultant in respective engineering field.

- You may sit in various competitive examinations viz. IES, IAS , MBA, and many more

- You may get promotions in many Government /Private/Public sector departments
