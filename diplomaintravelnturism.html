Diploma in travel and tourism

The travel & tourism industry is vibrant, committed to service excellence and professionalism. As more and more people, travel for business and leisure the industry has seen a demand for well trained, service sector professionals. The industry requires people who can deal competently with a wide range of activities and who possess the knowledge, skills and attributes required by the industry.


The course is designed to provide those wishing to enter this field with theoretical knowledge and practical ability in a range of skills and procedures necessary in the travel and tourism work roles.


Students to gain confidence in their ability to apply their knowledge and skills to function effectively/ competently in the Tourism & Travel industry also to enable them to be responsible for their own career growth / development.


Eligibility

Duration (1/2/3 Year's)

H.S.C OR Equivalent Examination (Any Faculty) (With English Subject Compulsory)


Future Scope

After completion of a course in any field of travel and tourism, many of the colleges provide placements in sectors such as hospitality, shipping, aviation and, of course, tourism. Some of the companies that attend such placement events in the colleges are Thomas Cook, Cox & Kings, Indian Railways Catering and Ticketing Corporation (IRCTC), SOTC Travel, Make My Trip, Le Passage to India Pvt Ltd, Snow Leopard Adventure, Pearls Tourism, Lufthansa Cargo, Fair Fest Media Ltd, Concord, Colourful Vacations, Zenith Hospitality, Southern Travels, Ziva Spa and Maxx Fun Holidays. Depending on the certificate or diploma or the specialization you pursued in the degree course, you can work in positions like tour operator, event manager, ticketing officer, adventure tourism expert, transport officer, holiday consultant and field officers. Other related division where professionals with a degree or diploma in travel and tourism can find openings are cargo services, logistics, transport agencies, airlines, hotels, and tourism departments in government and private concerns.
